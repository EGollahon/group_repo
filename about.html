The Coffee Shop began as a thought and transformed into a reality once Rachael, the owner, bought retail space in downtown Indianapolis. She renovated the space to feel warm and relaxing to all customers who visited. The once abandoned storefront now holds one of the cities most beloved shops. Come in today to be greeted by a smiling face, reflect upon the historic features, and enjoy a warm cup of coffee.
