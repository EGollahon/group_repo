About the Owner

Rachael owns The Coffee Shop in Indianapolis, Indiana. In her undergraduate years at IUPUI, where she majored in business, she spent her time studying with friends at various coffee shops. After graduation, she opened her own shop, hoping that other students and people in the community would be able to experience the comfort and joy of a neighborhood coffee shop.
